What is Gradselect?

Gradselect helps companies hire job-ready graduates faster. Candidates are pre-evaluated through assignments and interviews before being shared with companies.

How it works for Candidates

  1. Upload resume and skills
  2. Complete skill-based assignment
  3. Attend Gradselect interview
  4. Profile gets activated for suitable jobs
  5. Apply to verified company roles

How it works for Companies

  1. Receive pre-screened candidate profiles
  2. Get assignment + interview report
  3. Skip early hiring rounds
  4. Interview or hire directly

Why Companies Love Gradselect

  • Saves time and hiring cost
  • Only job-ready candidates
  • No upfront payment

Why Candidates Trust Gradselect

  • One assessment, multiple opportunities
  • Higher confidence after 2 rounds
  • Fair and skill-based hiring
